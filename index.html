About

Everyone has a story worth telling,
if you know how to look and listen.

To me, filmmaking is the art of turning quiet observations into meaningful stories.

My background in Geography and Social and Cultural Anthropology shapes the way I see the world. It taught me to look closer at how people live, how they connect, and what truly moves them.

Video journalism gave me a strong foundation in shaping stories with clarity and heart. Through camera work, visual storytelling, and editing, my aim is always to make complex subjects feel human and tangible.

I'm looking for the real moments that stay with you.
